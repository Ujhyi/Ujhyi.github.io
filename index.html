Bachelor Degree Project

Solving the real problem of Data Analytics


This thesis focuses on the prediction of NHL player salaries based on statistics obtained from hockey games (NHL). The main objective is to develop a model that can accurately predict a player's salary based on his performance on the ice.


I used 6 machine learning models to predict salary values. I used Linear Regression, K-Nearest Neighbors, Decision Trees, LightGB Model, Random Forest and Naive Bayes. The accuracy of the models was evaluated by R - square coefficient, Mean Squared Error and Mean Absolute Error.
